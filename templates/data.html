The raw VicRoads Crash Dataset for 2010-2015

The Dataset

All the data used in throughout this webapp was acquired from VicRoads. The original dataset contained 65 attributes, which after pre-processing steps involving feature selection, was reduced down to 44 attributes for analysis.

The attributes that are used for analysis are summarised in the table below.

Attribute Description
ACCIDENT_DATE The date the crash occured
ACCIDENT_TIME The time the crash occured at
ALCOHOLTIME If the time when the crash occured at is known to be a common time when people drink
ACCIDENT_TYPE The type of crash
DCA_CODE A description of the crash
HIT_RUN_FLAG If the crash was a hit and run
LIGHT_CONDITION The visibility on the road, if it was lit or if it was dark
POLICE_ATTEND If police attended the crash scene or not
ROAD_GEOMETRY If the crash occured at at intersection and the type of that intersection
SEVERITY If the crash causes injury, serious injury or fatality
SPEED_ZONE Speed zone the crash occured in
LONGITUDE Longitude coordinate of the crash
LATITUDE Latitude coordinate of the crash
LGA_NAME The name of the local government area the crash occured in
REGION_NAME The region the crash occured in
TOTAL_PERSONS Amount of people involved in the crash
INJ_OR_FATAL If the crash caused any injuries or fatalities
FATALITY The number of fatalities the caused caused
SERIOUSINJURY The number of serious injuries the crash caused
OTHERINJURY The number of other types of injruy the crash caused
NONINJURED The number of non-injured people in the crash
MALES Amount of males involved in the crash
FEMALES Amount of people female in the crash
BICYCLIST Amount of bicyclists involved in the crash
PASSENGER Amount of passengers involved in the crash
DRIVER Amount of drivers involved in the crash
PEDESTRIAN Amount of pedestrians involved in the crash
PILLION If the car crashed into a pillion
MOTORIST If the car crashed into another motorist
UNKNOWN Unknown reason for car crash
PED_CYCLIST_5_12 If the pedestrians/cyclists involved were between 5 - 12 years in age
PED_CYCLIST_13_18 If the pedestrians/cyclists involved were between 13 - 18 years in age
OLD_PEDESTRIAN If the pedestrian involved was old
OLD_DRIVER If the driver was old
YOUNG_DRIVER If the driver was young
ALCOHOL_RELATED If the crash was alcohol releated
UNLICENCSED If the vehicle in the crash was unlicensed
NO_OF_VEHICLES Amount of vehicles involved in the crash
HEAVYVEHICLE If the vehicle involved in the crash was a heavy vehical like a truck
PASSENGERVEHICLE If the vehicle involved in the crash was a passenger vehicle
MOTORCYCLE If there was a motor cycle invloved
PUBLICVEHICLE If the vehicle was a public vehicle, i.e. bus
DEG_URBAN_NAME The type of area that the crash was located in
